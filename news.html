Breaking News: Isak Transfer Saga Continues

Alexander Isak absent from Newcastle squad, expected to miss PFA Awards amid Liverpool interest

ISAK TRANSFER

Isak Refuses to Play for Newcastle

Alexander Isak did not feature for Newcastle in their Premier League opener and is not expected to attend tonight's PFA Awards. The striker insists he won't play for Newcastle again following strong interest from Liverpool.

Source: The Athletic, ESPN

LIVERPOOL SALES

Liverpool Pass £200m in Player Sales

Liverpool have surpassed £200m in player sales this summer following the £25m sale of Ben Doak to Bournemouth. Doak's move is the latest in a series of sales that have pushed Liverpool's revenue past the symbolic figure.

Source: ESPN

GARNACHO INTEREST

Chelsea Prepare Garnacho Bid

Manchester United are preparing for a bid from Chelsea for Alejandro Garnacho. The young Argentine is reportedly keen on a move to Stamford Bridge.

Source: Daily Mail, ESPN

PALACE DUO

Eze & Guehi in Transfer Spotlight

Tottenham are in a "one-horse race" to sign Eberechi Eze from Crystal Palace, while Liverpool are interested in Marc Guehi. Palace will only sell Eze if they can sign one or two forwards first.

Source: The Athletic, Daily Mail

WEEKEND RESULTS

Opening Weekend Results & Performances

Arsenal defeated Manchester United 1-0 at Old Trafford thanks to a Riccardo Calafiori goal. Manchester City opened with a 4-0 win over Wolves, while Leeds celebrated their Premier League return with a controversial 1-0 win over Everton.

Source: The National, Arseblog

UNITED GOALKEEPER CRISIS

Man United's Goalkeeper Crisis
