How to Use Paul Mitchell Clarifying Shampoo

  1. Wet hair thoroughly with warm water
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your palm
  3. Massage into your scalp and work through to the ends of your hair
  4. Rinse thoroughly
  5. Repeat if necessary for heavily soiled hair
  6. Follow with your favorite Paul Mitchell conditioner

Frequency: For best results, use once a week or as needed. Those with very dry hair may want to limit use to every two weeks.
