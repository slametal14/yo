How to Use Your Paul Mitchell Flat Iron

  1. Start with clean, completely dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum
  3. Set the temperature according to your hair type
  4. Section your hair for easier styling
  5. Take small sections (1-2 inches wide)
  6. Clamp the iron near roots and glide smoothly to ends
  7. Repeat until all hair is styled
  8. Finish with a light hairspray if desired

Pro Tip: For extra shine, run the iron over each section only once rather than multiple times.
